**Wiring Notes:**
- Each mortar group (6 mortars) is fused and connected to a single cue.
- Cakes are single-cue each.
- Use both channels evenly; do not exceed 18 cues per channel.
- Overlap cues by 10–20s for seamless transitions.
- Avenger cakes are distributed for variety.
- All items from inventory are used.
